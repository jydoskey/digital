Cedarview Communications Limited (CCL) is a rising force in the design, planning and implementation of innovative, technological solutions for a myriad of business and social challenges.

We provide exceptional service in the design and implementation of voice, data and video networks using proven cost-effective solutions through our strategic alliances with industry players that are committed to delivering such value.

OUR APPROACH

We undertake to understand our customer’s needs before coming up with a solution rather than a one-size fits all approach.

We are licensed to carry out the installation of terminal and network equipment by Nigerian Communications Commission

Our management team and staff are made up of some of the brightest heads and experienced hands in the industry. Their collective experience spans over 200 years in the deployment of telecommunication and IT networks across the oil & gas, banking and real estate market segments.

Our members of staff have worked on both sides of the table as service providers and as customers. This infuses them with empathy and a customer-centric approach in our engagements with clients.
